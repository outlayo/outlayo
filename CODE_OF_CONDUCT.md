# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

We want Outlayo to be a respectful, technically rigorous, and welcoming project for contributors of different backgrounds and experience levels.

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- being respectful and constructive in technical disagreement
- assuming good intent while giving clear feedback
- sharing context, evidence, and reproduction steps
- helping new contributors navigate the codebase and workflow

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- doxxing or sharing private information
- bad-faith arguments, trolling, or intentionally disruptive behavior
- publishing secrets, credentials, or customer-sensitive data

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, or restrict participation for behavior that violates this code of conduct.

To report conduct issues, contact: `conduct@outlayo.com`

## Scope

This applies to repository discussions, issues, pull requests, and other public project spaces.
